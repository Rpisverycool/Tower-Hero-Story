The Wizards Journey

(Keep in mind the maps may or may not be in order.) Hero Abilities: Dumpster Child: Burns enemies , Wizard shoots his wand at enemies, Chef: bonks enemies with hammer, Voca: shoots out a firework damaging enemies, Stella: creates paintings that do damage, Soda Pop: throws damaging soda bottles, Fracture: throws crystals damaging enemies, Scientist: makes a laser beam damaging enemies, Slime King: summons slimes dealing damage to enemies In Its way. Lemonade Cat: farms mana. Beebo: throws Ice cream at enemies, Spectre: boosts nearby heroes damage but Increase cost of upgrades, Keith: throws a boomerang damaging enemies. Balloon Pal: Buffs range but decreases attack rate. Discount Dog: decreases price of heroes but lowers damage. Maitake: poisons enemies.
